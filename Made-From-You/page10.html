Made From You

We didn’t actually store your data here. But for next time, remember:

WHAT WE LEARNED

  • How fast you react when something looks urgent.


  • When you click without reading the small text.


  • Which buttons your eyes are drawn to first.


  • How long you hesitate before making a choice.


  • Whether you accept everything, or try to review first.

WHAT YOU CAN DO

  • Slow down before clicking “Accept” or “Continue”.


  • Look for dark patterns: timers, giant bright buttons, tiny “No”.


  • Use privacy tools (ad blockers, tracker blockers) when you can.


  • Install VPN's when needed


  • Remember: your behaviour is often more valuable than your name.
